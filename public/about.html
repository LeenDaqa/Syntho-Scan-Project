About Us

Our Team: The hardworking group behind SynthoScan CT is pleased to welcome you. We are a trio of passionate students at Princess Sumaya University for Technology. We have worked together throughout our academic journey to present you this innovative project that combines our skills, passion, and abilities.

Our Goal: Our goal is to produce accurate synthetic CTs from MRIs using advanced Deep Learning. This innovation improves precision in dose calculations, streamlines workflows, and contributes to personalized cancer treatments. Come along with us as we use innovative technology to help patients' cancer treatment journeys go more smoothly!
